About Athena

Athena Fact Checker is an AI-powered browser extension that helps you verify information across the web. It uses advanced natural language processing to analyze claims and cross-reference them with reliable sources.

Features:

  • Real-time fact-checking of selected text
  • Automatic page content analysis
  • Source verification and citation
  • Confidence scoring for fact-check results
  • History tracking and export
